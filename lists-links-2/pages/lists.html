Five Terms Regarding A Hobby

Wall Cloud
A lowering of the storm cell or cumulonimbus.
Hook/Hook Echo
A pattern on a radar image that shows a "hook" in the SW location of the storm, which indicates extreme rotation or tornadic activity.
Scud Cloud
Scud clouds are sometimes confused with tornado, but are actually the remains of a storm system that are lingered behind.
Microburst
Sometimes confused with tornadic winds, downforce winds that are pushed ahead of a storms frontal edge.
Tornado
A column of air that rotates with destructive speed and force; must be connected from the base of the storm to the ground. Often times they are funnel clouds.
